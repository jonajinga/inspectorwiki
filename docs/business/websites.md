# Websites

A professional website is the foundation of your online presence. It establishes credibility, captures leads, and provides a 24/7 booking channel.

## Essential Pages

- **Home** — clear value proposition, service area, and prominent booking CTA
- **Services** — detailed descriptions of each inspection type offered
- **About** — credentials, experience, and team bios
- **Service Areas** — individual pages for each city or region you serve (critical for local SEO)
- **Contact/Booking** — online scheduling integration (Calendly, Spectora, etc.)

## SEO Basics

- Claim and optimize your Google Business Profile
- Target location-specific keywords ("home inspector in [city]")
- Generate reviews — Google weighs them heavily for local rankings
- Ensure mobile responsiveness and fast load times

:::tip
Each service area page should have unique content about that location — not just the city name swapped in a template. Google penalizes thin, duplicate content.
:::
