# Payment Processing

Getting paid promptly and professionally is essential. Most clients expect to pay by credit card, and many will pay at the time of booking or on-site.

## Payment Processors

| Processor | Fee | Best For |
|-----------|-----|----------|
| Square | 2.6% + $0.10 | On-site card reader |
| Stripe | 2.9% + $0.30 | Online payments, invoicing |
| PayPal | 2.99% + $0.49 | Client familiarity |
| Inspection software (Spectora, etc.) | Varies | Integrated invoicing |

## Best Practices

- Collect payment at time of booking or before delivering the report
- Send invoices immediately after inspection — delays reduce collection rates
- Accept multiple methods: card, ACH/bank transfer, and digital wallets
- Include clear payment terms in your pre-inspection agreement

:::warning
Avoid collecting payment in cash without providing a receipt. Always maintain a paper trail for tax and liability purposes.
:::
