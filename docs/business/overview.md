# Business & Marketing Overview

Building a successful inspection business requires more than technical knowledge. Marketing, client relations, software tools, and business operations are all essential.

## Getting Started

1. **Licensing** — research your state's requirements (education hours, exam, insurance)
2. **Insurance** — E&O (errors and omissions) and general liability are standard
3. **Software** — choose inspection report software (Spectora, HomeGauge, Palm-Tech, etc.)
4. **Equipment** — moisture meter, electrical tester, gas leak detector, ladder, flashlight, camera

## Marketing Channels

- **Real estate agent relationships** — the primary referral source for most inspectors
- **Google Business Profile** — essential for local search visibility
- **Website** — professional site with service areas, pricing transparency, and booking
- **Reviews** — actively collect Google and Yelp reviews after every inspection

:::tip
Focus on building relationships with 10–15 active real estate agents in your area. Consistent, reliable service generates more referrals than any advertising budget.
:::
