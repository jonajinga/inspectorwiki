# Advertising

Effective advertising for inspectors combines digital presence with relationship-based marketing. The most successful inspectors diversify across multiple channels.

## Google Ads

Google Local Services Ads (LSAs) are the most effective paid channel for inspectors. You pay per lead, not per click, and your ad appears at the top of search results with a "Google Guaranteed" badge.

## Realtor Outreach

Real estate agents are the primary referral source for most inspectors. Build relationships through:

- Lunch-and-learn presentations at brokerages
- Dropping off branded materials (cards, brochures, magnets)
- Quick turnaround times and professional reports
- CE class sponsorship

## Print Marketing

- Door hangers in target neighborhoods
- Direct mail to recently listed properties
- Sponsorship of local community events
- Business cards with QR codes linking to your booking page

:::tip
Track where every lead comes from. Ask "How did you hear about us?" on your booking form and review the data quarterly to optimize your spend.
:::
