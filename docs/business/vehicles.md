# Vehicles

Your vehicle is a mobile billboard and a practical tool. The right vehicle with professional branding builds trust before you even knock on the door.

## Vehicle Selection

Most inspectors drive SUVs, trucks, or crossovers — vehicles that can carry a ladder, tools, and handle unpaved driveways. Popular choices include the Toyota 4Runner, Ford Explorer, and Chevrolet Colorado.

## Vehicle Wraps

A full or partial wrap is one of the highest-ROI marketing investments for a local service business.

- **Full wrap** — $2,500–$5,000, maximum visibility
- **Partial wrap** — $1,000–$2,500, covers key panels
- **Magnets or decals** — $100–$500, removable option

Include your company name, phone number, website, and a clear description of services. Keep the design clean and readable at 30+ mph.

:::warning
Check local sign ordinances — some HOAs and municipalities regulate commercial vehicle parking in residential areas.
:::
