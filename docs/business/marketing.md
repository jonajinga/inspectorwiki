# Marketing Strategy

Marketing for inspection companies revolves around one goal: being the inspector that agents and homebuyers think of first. A balanced strategy uses both online and offline channels.

## Online Marketing

- **Website and SEO** — Your website is your 24/7 salesperson. Optimize for local search terms like "home inspector in [city]."
- **Google Business Profile** — Claim and optimize it. Reviews here directly influence your local pack ranking.
- **Email campaigns** — Monthly newsletters to your real estate agent contact list keep you top of mind without being pushy.

## Offline Marketing

- **Agent office visits** — Drop off business cards and introduce yourself to real estate offices in your service area.
- **Lunch-and-learns** — Host a short educational session at a brokerage on common inspection findings.
- **Local sponsorships** — Community events and youth sports put your name in front of homeowners.

## Tracking ROI

Ask every client how they found you and log the answer. Over time, this data reveals which channels actually produce revenue versus which feel productive but generate nothing.

:::tip
Allocate 5–10% of gross revenue to marketing. Track cost per lead by channel quarterly and cut what does not convert.
:::
