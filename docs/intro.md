# Welcome to InspectorWiki

InspectorWiki is a free, community-driven knowledge base covering every aspect of property inspection — from foundations to roofing, standards to business practices.

## Who is this for?

- **New inspectors** studying for certification or building foundational knowledge
- **Working inspectors** looking for quick reference on systems, defects, and codes
- **Home buyers and sellers** who want to understand inspection reports
- **Educators and trainers** developing inspection curriculum

## How to use the wiki

Browse by topic using the sidebar, or use the search bar to find specific content. Each article covers:

- **System overview** — what it is and how it works
- **Common defects** — what to look for during inspection
- **Standards references** — applicable codes and standards of practice
- **Reporting guidance** — how to document and communicate findings

## Contributing

InspectorWiki is open source. You can contribute by:

- **Writing articles** — share your expertise on a system, defect, or technique
- **Editing content** — fix errors, improve clarity, add references
- **Suggesting topics** — [open an issue on GitHub](https://github.com/jonajinga/inspectorwiki/issues)
