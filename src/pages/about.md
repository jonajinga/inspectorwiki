InspectorWiki is a free, community-driven encyclopedia covering every aspect of property inspection. Our goal is to make inspection knowledge accessible to everyone — from students studying for their first certification to veterans with decades of field experience.

## Why InspectorWiki?

The property inspection industry lacks a centralized, open knowledge base. Training materials are locked behind paywalls, scattered across forums, or buried in outdated textbooks. InspectorWiki changes that.

## How to Contribute

InspectorWiki is open source. You can contribute by:

- **Writing articles** — share your expertise on a system, defect, or inspection technique
- **Editing existing content** — fix errors, improve clarity, add references
- **Suggesting topics** — open an issue on GitHub with topics you'd like to see covered
